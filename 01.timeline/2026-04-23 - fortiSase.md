~~~ Additional Information ~~~~

For Commerce Bank, the benefits of SASE are significant:

• Zero Trust Network Access (ZTNA) — Replace legacy VPN with identity-aware, least-privilege access that verifies every user and device before granting access to sensitive financial systems.
• Unified Policy Management — FortiSASE integrates natively with FortiManager, meaning your team manages cloud and on-prem security from a single pane of glass, reducing operational complexity and the risk of policy drift.
• Enhanced Visibility & Compliance — Your FortiAnalyzer investment carries forward, giving you consolidated logging and reporting across your entire environment — critical for meeting regulatory requirements like PCI-DSS and SOX.
• Reduced Total Cost of Ownership — Rather than layering in a separate SASE vendor, FortiSASE runs on your existing Fortinet licensing model, protecting your current investment.
