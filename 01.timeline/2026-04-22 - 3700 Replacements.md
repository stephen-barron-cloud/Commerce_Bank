Key takeaways:

The FG-3701F delivers 4x the NGFW throughput, nearly 6x the threat protection, 2.8x the concurrent sessions, and 2.3x the new sessions/sec compared to the FG-3700D.
The FG-3701F moves from the older NP6/CP8 ASIC generation to the newer NP7/CP9, which accounts for the dramatic performance jump.
The FG-3701F is also more compact at 2U vs 3U, despite significantly higher performance.
The FG-3701F supports much higher-speed interfaces (up to 400GE QSFP-DD) vs the FG-3700D's maximum of 40GE QSFP+.
